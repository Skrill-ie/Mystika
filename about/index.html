A History of
Cafe Mystika

Nestled in the tranquil landscapes of Valencia, Negros Oriental, Cafe Mystika was born out of a passion for coffee, community, and sustainability. Recognizing the need for a space where locals and tourists alike could enjoy high-quality beverages while unwinding in nature, the idea for the café was set into motion. Cafe Mystika Through Time

Inception and Conceptualization
The concept for Cafe Mystika began with a vision of creating a haven for coffee lovers and adventurers. The idea was to offer an escape from the hustle and bustle of urban life, blending sustainability with creativity in beverage offerings.
Establishment
Cafe Mystika opened its doors in Valencia, Negros Oriental, with a menu celebrating local and international coffee. From day one, the café offered a space that catered to everyone, from solo travelers seeking solitude to families enjoying a peaceful outing.
Expansion of Offerings
Building on its success, Cafe Mystika expanded its menu to include a diverse range of beverages and complementary products to suit every preference, ensuring its inclusivity.
Community Initiatives
Cafe Mystika began actively engaging with the local community through sustainability projects, collaborations with local farmers and artisans, and events that brought people together, reflecting its ethos of serving all walks of life.
Recognition as a Local and Tourist Favorite
Known for its innovative beverages and serene ambiance, the café became a sought-after destination for both locals and tourists seeking quality and peace of mind.
Sustainability Milestones
Staying true to its mission, Cafe Mystika achieved significant milestones in adopting sustainable practices, from eco-friendly packaging to sourcing ethically and locally.
Looking Ahead
Cafe Mystika continues to evolve, staying at the forefront of the coffee industry while remaining grounded in its mission to unite people and provide a timeless experience for all.
